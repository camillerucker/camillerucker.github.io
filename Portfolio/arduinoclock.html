Arduino Clock Arduino, Adobe-Illustrator, Laser Cutter

Description

This laser cut, Arduino powered clock provides has an aesthetically pleasing laser cut frame. It icorporates both analog and digital features with 12 LED's lining the perimeter to denote hours, and a rotating wheel in the center to denote seconds. Using the control panel, the time can be adjusted, and an alarm can set.

Technical

The Base was designed by Adobe Illustrator and cut by a laser cutter at TechShop.The Arduino controls all 12 hour LED, 14 Inputs for the two 7-segment displays, and the 4 switches/buttons, using 4 daisy chained shift registers (8 outputs each). Next time, instead of using a breadboard for all these outputs, I will custom print a PCB. The seconds dial in the center is rotated by a Servo.
