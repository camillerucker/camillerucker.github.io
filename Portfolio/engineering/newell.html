Newell Brands Internship

Description

During the summer of 2018, I interned in the Concept Development Engineering group at the Newell Brands Design Center. Working for brands including Graco, Crockpot, and Calphalon, I designed and built functional prototypes based off sketches from Industrial Designers. My work was focused in mechanism developement. I also participated in brainstorm sessions for new product ideas and worked closely with a cross functional team. For more information about specific projects and examples of work, please contact me.
