iStitch Java, C++, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Firebase

Description

iStitch is easy to use online image processing software that allows doctors to upload videos of the corneal sub-basal nerve plexus. I stitch together hundreds of frames of the video, and then make a tree of vectors from the veins. This tree allows stats such as CNFT(fiber tortuosity), CNBD (branch density), CNFD (fiber desnity), and CNFL (fiber length) to be computed. These stats differ healty patients and patiets with disorders such as diabetes, thus disorders can autonomously be found.

I worked with Yash Vaishnav, an aspiring doctor at Quinnipiac University. We are currently working on publishing a paper
