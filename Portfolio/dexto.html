Dexto Web, Javascript, Neural-Networks

Description

Dexto, winner of the Myo award at AngelHack Silicon Valley, reads raw emg data from a Myo Armband and calculates finger positions of a hand. I worked on the algorithms which use a baysian classifier to determine finger position, and double integrating accelerometer readings to get position. You can get a demonstration of the accuracy from our 3D Viewer.

Dexto's API allows other developers to get the finger angles after Dexto's processing. The Myo is much cheaper than other alternatives for amputees to control their prosthethetic, thus Dexto would be a good choice for a prosthetic arm to get angles. We demonstrated the concept with the 3D model.
