Description

I am the head of Democracy of Sound (DOS), a club which strives to bring people together through technology and Music. My Interactive arduino midi keypad was showcased at in(fest)us, one of DOS's festivals.

Technical

The Arduino Teensy 2.0 ++ sends MIDI signals to a computer running Ableton, musical editing software. Ableton processes and applies effects to the Midi Signals from the Arduino. A beat always plays in the background. A knob adjusts the pitch of the beat, and a distance sensor adjusts the pace. The closer people walk, the faster the beat. There is a 4x4 button Matrix which plays the melody notes (C, C#, F,G,G# for a creepy affect)
